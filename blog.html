Lazada

Lazada eLogistic Entity is Lazada Group subsidiary has crucial mission among Lazada Group journey regarding Logistic System.

We built this project from scratch following Micro-service architect in which we centralize all business logic over 5 platforms: Warehouse System, Third Party System, Operation System, Stock/User Service, Transfer Package System.

We confront millions requests per hours to distribute Packages and process Orders in 6 countries.

I myself figured out some key points:

+ Test Driven Development: we don't have time to do it twice. We need to spend enough time to do it correctly for first time in order to make it as our Safeguard to protect our business logic

+ Sequential and Parallel processing: when it comes to e-commerce, there are millions to billions unit of work must be done a day. Some of those must be processed sequentially and simultaneously. Workflow Pattern is one of the best approach to tune them into perfect way.

+ Specification Document must take into account exchange information among micro-services. It means behaviors and exchange information of every single services must be clear enough to write Test-Case.
